Tips & Habits

Small habits create big results.

Build confidence with habits that protect your body and your motivation. These tips focus on consistency, recovery, and sustainable progress.

Consistency First

Choose 3 workout days and protect them like appointments. Consistency matters more than intensity early on.

Tip: Pair workouts with a routine cue like morning coffee or lunch break.

Recover to Improve

Muscles grow during recovery. Sleep, hydration, and rest days help you return stronger.

Tip: Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep and at least one full rest day each week.

Train Safely

Start with light weights, focus on form, and increase loads slowly. Your joints will thank you.

Tip: If you cannot control the weight, it is too heavy for now.
